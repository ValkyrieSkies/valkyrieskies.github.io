[Article] Tribes 3's AI Imagery Debacle

A selection of the AI-generated banner images featured in Tribes 3: Rivals, including the infamous "spaceship-sniper."

With the March 7th playtest update a number of cosmetic banner images[1] were added to the game which could be unlocked via progressing achievements. While some of these were [pretty solid quality renders](https://wiki.tribesdepot.com/w/images/7/7c/T3R-Achievement_Background-Nova_Colt_Master_II.png), members of the community very quickly noticed that the majority of them were AI-generated images, and rather poor ones at that, with the most infamous example being the [Frag Artist V banner](https://wiki.tribesdepot.com/wiki/Tribes_3:_Rivals/Achievements), which features a bizarre misshapen amalgamation of a sniper rifle and a spaceship. Some people initially made posts praising the banners, not recognizing them for what they were ([with lead designer Erez seemingly leaning into that ignorance]()), but once the community became aware a [Discord forum post]() was made in short order by user 'theciv' to seek further explanation from Prophecy and confirm if they were simply temporary placeholders. Prophecy Team member 'doppleganger123' would [confirm]() in that forum thread that generative AI ***was*** used to create a number of the images, and further went on to [elaborate that the majority of it was planned to remain in the game as-is]().

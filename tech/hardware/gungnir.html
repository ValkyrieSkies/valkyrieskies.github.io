[Hardware] Desktop PC - "Gungnir"

My primary computer for both work and relaxation alike. Built just around the start of the pandemic, I was lucky enough to purchase most of the parts right before they all shot up in price. Though it wasn't the strongest build going, the Ryzen 5 3600X and Radeon RX 570 put in years of solid service that rarely saw me wanting for more until I started dabbling in VR. Now with a powerful combo of the Ryzen 5700X3D and Radeon RX 7900 GRE installed, I hope it'll continue to serve as my trusty partner for many more years to come.

Gungnir Mk.IV

Operating System: Windows 10 Home
Case: Corsair 275R Airflow (ATX Mid-Tower)
Motherboard: Asus TUF GAMING X570-PLUS WI-FI (ATX)
CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 5700X3D
CPU Cooler: Thermalright Phantom Spirit 120 EVO
GPU: Sapphire Pulse Radeon RX 7900 GRE
RAM: Corsair Vengeance LPX 32GB (2x16GB) DDR4-3200
Storage (OS): Samsung 870 QVO 1TB SSD (2.5" SATA)
Storage (Bulk): Crucial BX500 2TB SSD (2.5" SATA)
